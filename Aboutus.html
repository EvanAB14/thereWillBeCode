About Us

Pick_up_sports - Helping others to Create and join games in Local areas

Pick_up_sports is a fast-growing application that keeps culture and creativity at the heart of everything we do. Our mission is to help custorners unlock their creativity and recreational activities and build events and join Games using our uniquely powerful design platform and our tirelessly helpful support.

View our work

Who are we

Kelcie Mitchelmore, Kiranjot Kaur, Evan Boileau, Luca Rojas, Don McLean Our mission is to help people unlock their creativity and to improve their physical, mental, and social health and well-being by participating in group sports and recreational activities at the community level.

What We Do

Our application, "pick_up_sports", helps individuals and groups Create and Participate in sports and recreational activities in their Local areas using our uniquely powerful design platform and tireless helpful support. Current sports offerings include: hockey, baseball, basketball, soccer, and tennis. Current locations are in the Greater Toronto Area (GTA), Toronto, Canada.
